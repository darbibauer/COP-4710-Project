    Ever walk into the kitchen and stare at the food like, "What am I supposed to make with any of this?" We'd like to offer you a website where you can plan your own meals. When you sign up as a member, you get some added features. On the left is a list of features which are available on this website. The features you have access to without signing up are links in which you can use to access them. Those features not made into links are features you could have access to by signing up.

    Click the Profile link to the left to go to your profile page. On your profile page you can edit your personal information or add a recipe to your grocery list. You can also just click Add Recipe to the left to go directly to the Recipe Form. The Search Recipes link to the left will take you to a search page that will allow you to search for different recipes. We will also pair those dishes with recommended dishes, in addition to some side dishes you may enter that you would like to pair with the main dish. You can search from a broad category (like breakfast or Mexican), a specific main dish (tacos), or specific keywords (gluten free, spicey, pasta). You can loggout directly from the main site or from within your profile page.
